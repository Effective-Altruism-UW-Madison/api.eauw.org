New to Effective Altruism?
For people who want to change the world for the better, effective altruism offers a framework for comparing different ways to do good and then acting on the most promising of them. Effective altruism is both a research field and a community of people dedicated to using high-quality evidence and careful reasoning to work out how to help others as much as possible. 

If you are interested in preventing pandemics and nuclear wars, helping farmed animals, giving to effective charities, the risks of artificial intelligence, ethics, evidence-based discussions, or just in doing good, then this organization is for you!
