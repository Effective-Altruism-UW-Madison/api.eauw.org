New to Effective Altruism?
The world faces so many problems that it can be difficult to know where to start. For people who want to change the world, effective altruism (EA) offers a framework for comparing different ways to do good and then acting on the most promising of them. Effective altruism is both a research field and a community of people, dedicated to using evidence and reasoning to work out how to help others the most.
